Rooted in the classical university tradition, arts and humanities degrees examine the role that human beings play in the development of the world. arts and humanities majors look at the ways that art and culture have influenced history, and how those decisions affect the way we deal with other people in our everyday lives.

Applied science is the use of existing scientific knowledge to practical goals, like technology or inventions. Within natural science, disciplines that are basic science develop basic information to explain and perhaps predict phenomena in the natural world.
