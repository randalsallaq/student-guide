Architecture

What is Architecture?

You must have been impressed by many spectacular construction works like the Duomo di Milano cathedral or maybe contemporary buildings such as Heydar Alive center of Baku. Architecture is the studying of how forms and structures convey the building. Historical civilizations are often identified with their surviving architectural achievements. Architecture develops over time, adopting to humans needs and thier cultural diversity.


Why to study Architecture?

The type of person an architect is the aesthitec oriented and the one who is interested in innovation works. An architect spends his daily life on sketching and visualising spaces. you need to come up with neat ideas. Daily tasks also combine working with drawing and BIM software and he occassionally do site visits.If you find you have high imaginary and conceptual abilities then go for it.
