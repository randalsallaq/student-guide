What is Architecture?

You must have been impressed by many spectacular construction works like the Duomo di Milano cathedral or maybe contemporary buildings such as Heydar Alive center of Baku. Indeed it's a breathtaking works that were accomplished in the grace of a perfect art and science mix. It's the studying of how forms and structures can express the building. Historical civilizations are often identified with their surviving architectural achievements. Architecture develops over time, adopting to humans needs and thier cultural diversity.

Did you know?

THE PARTHENON HAS BEEN A TEMPLE, A CHURCH, AND A MOSQUE THROUGHOUT IT'S LIFETIME. What is the Parthenon? The Parthenon's been fought over during the centuries by different religions. It started out as a temple dedicated to the Greek goddess Athena. Then, it was taken over by Christians and converted into a church, then it was taken over by the Turks and transitioned to a mosque with a minaret erected over it.

The functionaliy of the building may shape up the design process. According to the Roman architect Vitruvius a good building should satisfy the three principles of firmitas, utilitas, venustas which translates to:
Durability: a building should stand up robustly and remain in good condition.
Utility: it should be suitable for the purposes for which it is used.
Beauty: it should be aesthetically pleasing.
