Arabic Language

What is Arabic Language

Arabic language, Southern-Central Semitic language spoken in a large area including North Africa, most of the Arabian Peninsula, and other parts of the Middle East. Arabic language is one of the most important languages in the world nowadays. Although people think that this language is not important anymore but the reality says something different.


Why Study Arabic Language?

Arabic is the fourth most spoken language in the world. About 315 million people in 58 countries speak the Arabic as their first language, according to Ethnologue. Learning Arabic will make you stand out, as there are very few people from the West that speak Arabic. Having command of Arabic will make you appear clever and sophisticated. I
